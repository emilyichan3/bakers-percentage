What's baker's percentage?

Baker's percentage expresses the ratio of ingredients to flour in a bread recipe. Flour is always 100%, and other ingredients are calculated as a percentage of the flour weight. For example, if flour is 500g and water is 300g, water is 60% (300/500*100) of the flour.
